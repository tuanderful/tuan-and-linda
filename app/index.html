The day we met

I first noticed Linda at church. But it was church, so I had to pray extra hard and pretend to not pay attention to her. She eventually introduced herself to me, and I remember thinking, "she totally has the hots for me." She actually didn't.

I have never stopped trying to prove that nothing is impossible.
